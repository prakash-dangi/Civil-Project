Youth's Modulus: Theory and Formulas

Young's Modulus (E) measures the stiffness of a material. It is also known as the modulus of elasticity. It represents the ratio of stress to strain in a material under deformation. The formula is given by:

\( E = \frac{{\text{{Stress}}}}{{\text{{Strain}}}} \)

Young's Modulus is also referred to as the elastic modulus or the tensile modulus.

Shear Modulus (G): Theory and Formulas

Shear Modulus (G), also known as Modulus of Rigidity, measures the material's response to shear stress. The formula is given by:

\( G = \frac{{\text{{Shear Stress}}}}{{\text{{Shear Strain}}}} \)

Bulk Modulus: Theory and Formulas

Bulk Modulus (K) measures the material's response to uniform pressure. The formula is given by:

\( K = -V \frac{{dP}}{{dV}} \)
